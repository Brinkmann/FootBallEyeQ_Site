Individual Coach
Start here
Plan sessions with plastic cones now; unlock smart EyeQ cones next.

1.
Sign up free at football-eyeq.com/signup to access the drill catalogue, one session planner, and up to 10 favourites.
2.
Build your first session in the Session Planner and run it with plastic conesâ€”this teaches the same scanning principles as EyeQ hardware.
3.
To preview Premium during early access, email obrinkmann@gmail.com for a free upgrade. Premium unlocks the full 12-session planner, unlimited favourites, and stats.
4.
When EyeQ cones arrive, switch exercise type in the Drill Catalogue to EyeQ to add smart-light progressions; plastic drills remain available anytime.
Note: EyeQ exercises need the smart LED cones; Plastic exercises mirror the same cognitive demands using standard cones.
Club-managed premium

Club Coach (with code)
Start here
Use your club access code to unlock full-season tools immediately.

1.
Create a free coach account at football-eyeq.com/signup and tick the club box during signup.
2.
Redeem your access code on the Join Club page; the code typically arrives from your club admin by email.
3.
After redemption, head to the Session Planner for full 12-week planning, Drill Catalogue for EyeQ and Plastic drills, and Stats for usage insights.
Note: Your club admin controls who receives codes and can adjust drill permissions across the staff.
Free until code arrives

Club Coach (without code)
Start here
Start free, request your club invite, then unlock premium once your code is issued.

1.
Sign up free at football-eyeq.com/signup and begin with plastic drills and one session planner.
2.
Email your club admin the address you used to sign up so they can generate a matching code in their Club Dashboard.
3.
Once you receive the code, redeem it at football-eyeq.com/join-club to unlock full club benefits and keep your existing sessions.
Note: Codes are email-specificâ€”share the exact signup email with your admin to avoid delays.
Manage coaches & access

Club Admin
Start here
Create and distribute access codes, set drill permissions, and self-upgrade to review premium tools.

1.
Register your club at football-eyeq.com/club/signup and log into the Club Dashboard.
2.
Generate invite codes per coach email inside the dashboard, then share codes via email; coaches redeem at Join Club.
3.
Use the dashboard controls to manage memberships and decide if staff can access EyeQ drills or stick to team/Plastic content.
4.
Apply a self-upgrade to experience the premium journey yourselfâ€”start a sample season in the Planner and view Stats to spot progression gaps.
Note: Share codes promptly so coaches keep their plans synced; you can revoke or regenerate codes anytime in the dashboard.
