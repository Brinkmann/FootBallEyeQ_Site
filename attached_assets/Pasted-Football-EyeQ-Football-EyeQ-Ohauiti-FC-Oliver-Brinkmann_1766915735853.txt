002 - Dribbling Through Chaos

Players dribble freely inside a marked area, each with their own ball and wearing one of three bib colours (red, blue, or green). EyeQ SmartCones positioned around the perimeter cycle through colours, and players must scan to find a cone matching their bib colour while maintaining close control in traffic. When they reach a matching cone, they perform a turn and dribble back into space, continuously searching for their next target. This develops dribbling control, spatial awareness in crowded areas, and the habit of lifting the head to scan while moving with the ball.

038 - Triple-Task Switch Game

A high-intensity positional possession game where players must constantly scan both sidelines to identify which tactical task is currently live, as indicated by a specific pair of EyeQ smart cones lighting up. Each colour corresponds to a different game rule: hold possession patiently, switch play across the field, or attack directly. The challenge is to adapt decision-making, tempo, and team shape based solely on the visual cue, which operates independently of player actions. The mirrored cue layout ensures players develop full-field scanning habits under pressure, replicating the real-game need to check multiple directions before acting.

039 - Four-Sided Breakout with EyeQ Cues
